WHO WE ARE

Allied Microbiota (AMB) is a biotechnology company spun off from Columbia University focusing on solving environmental problems. We develop microbes and innovative bio-processes to destroy pollutants in soils and sediments, reduce the amount of carbon dioxide in the atmosphere, and create energy efficient industrial chemicals.

SOLUTIONS AT LOWER COST

We provide quick, scalable, safe, cost effective and energy efficient products for remediation of toxic organic pollutants.

SUSTAINABLE PRODUCTS

Our products use less energy and have sustainable impact on the environment


CUTTING EDGE RESEARCH

Our patented technology has shown to have 'best in class' performance. Our Technology is a result of decades of research by Columbia University Scientists.

We are working in synergy towards sustainable environmental waste management solutions.

Using microbial engineering to clean up our environment

Remediation of soil, sediments and groundwater using advanced Bio Process

Our patented microbial and enzymes cultures are capable of degrading PCBs, Dioxins PAHs, , BTEX among others in soil and sediments and 1,4 Dioxane in groundwater
