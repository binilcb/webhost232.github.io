ABOUT US

Allied Microbiota is a biotechnology firm that develops innovative solutions for remediation and biochemical industries. We were founded by professionals from Columbia University and work in close collaboration with Columbia University faculty. AMB’s mission is to use biotechnology and synthetic biology to clean up toxic pollutants in soil, sediments, and water by reducing the cost, energy, and environmental impact of cleanup efforts. We are a privately held firm based out of New York. We are currently targeting remediation and we have made substantial progress in the scale-up and development of our main bioremediation product with major environmental engineering firms Our approach is scalable to large sites that have been not been commercially feasible to remediate previously.
