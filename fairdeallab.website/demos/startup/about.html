REMEDIATION

While the environmental remediation industry has expanded significantly in the past few decades, its capabilities remain far below that needed to reduce the inventory of contaminated soils and sediments. There are over 1,300 sites on the National Priorities List (NPL) of the U.S Environmental Protection Agency and approximately 11 million people in the U.S., including 3-4 million children, live within 1 mile of a federal Superfund site and confront significant health risks. The remediation industry continually searches for more cost-effective technologies and the bacterial approach developed by AMB represents a breakthrough that the industry recognizes as having a significant potential.

Our Bioremediation Products use patented naturally isolated bacterial cultures and enzymes for remediation of soil, sediments and groundwater using techniques like composting, bio reactors and flow through reactors. Our aerobic microbes offer advantages to existing anaerobic/aerobic treatment trains for recalcitrant remediation. Extracted from natural environments and isolated under controlled laboratory conditions, these microbes destroy contamination at its source. Complete remediation often occurs in a matter of weeks or months versus several years or more for other methods, giving users the combined benefit of fast site closure and remarkable cost savings. Bioremediation with our products is effective as a stand-alone technology or may be used to augment an existing treatment method. Once we help determine that our microbes are compatible with your site conditions, they can be applied in-situ or ex-situ in a very straight-forward approach.
AMB’s new microbial solution can clean up difficult to remediate recalcitrant like PCBs, PAHs, dioxins, and emerging organic pollutants like 1,4 Dioxane at significantly lower cost in an energy-efficient way. Our solutions are quick, scalable, safe, cost effective and energy efficient. We have partnered with top environmental engineering firms and are currently working on large-scale pilot tests in early 2018.

Dioxins and Furans: Our products reduce dioxin levels by 60% in 6 days based on lab and scale up experiments.
PCBs:Our products reduce PCB levels by 50% in 6 days based on lab and scale up experiments.
PAHs:Our product reduces PAHs rapidly. The most toxic PAH, BaP was reduced by 95% in 48 hrs.
BTEX::Our products have been successful in decreasing the concentration of the BTEX components (Benzene, Toluene, Ethylbenzene and Xylene).
1,4-Dioxane:Our product reduces 1,4-D rapidly
